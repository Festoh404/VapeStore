Returns & Exchanges

Our straightforward policy to ensure you shop with confidence.

E-Liquids, Disposables & Pods

Due to health, safety, and hygiene regulations, we cannot accept returns or exchanges on e-liquids, disposable vapes, replacement pods, or coils once they have left our premises or the courier has delivered them. This applies even if the packaging is unopened. Please double-check your flavor and nicotine strength before completing your order!

Vape Hardware (Kits & Mods)

We offer a 48-hour Dead on Arrival (DOA) guarantee on all vape hardware. If your device is defective straight out of the box (e.g., won't turn on, auto-firing), please contact us immediately. We will exchange the defective unit for a new one after a quick inspection.

Note: This does not cover damage caused by user error, dropping the device, or water damage.

I Received the Wrong Item

If we accidentally sent you the wrong item, we sincerely apologize! Please do not open the product. Contact our support team within 24 hours of receiving your delivery, and we will dispatch a rider to swap the incorrect item for the right one at zero cost to you.

How to Initiate a Return

To report a DOA device or a mixed-up order, please call us or send a WhatsApp message to our customer service line immediately. Be sure to have your order number ready and, if possible, a short video or photo of the issue so we can sort it out for you in record time.
