Delivery Information

Fast, discreet, and reliable shipping across Nairobi and the rest of Kenya.

Nairobi Same-Day Delivery

We offer lightning-fast delivery within Nairobi and its environs. Orders placed between 8:00 AM and 6:00 PM are typically delivered right to your door within 1 to 2 hours.

Delivery fee: Calculated at checkout based on your exact location (typically KSh 200 - KSh 400).

Countrywide Shipping (G4S / Fargo)

Not in Nairobi? No problem. We ship countrywide via trusted couriers like G4S and Fargo Courier. Orders placed before 4:00 PM are dispatched the exact same day for Next-Day Delivery to your nearest courier office.

Standard countrywide shipping fee: KSh 400.

Discreet Packaging

We value your privacy. All orders are packed securely in plain, unbranded courier flyers or boxes. There are no vape-related logos, images, or obvious descriptions on the outside of your package.

Payment Methods

For Nairobi deliveries, we accept M-PESA on Delivery or Cash when the rider arrives. For countrywide courier shipments, payment for the items and shipping must be completed via M-PESA before the package is dispatched from our store.
